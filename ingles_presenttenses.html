English - Present Tenses ⏳

1. Choose the correct form to complete the sentence:
"She _______ (study) English every morning."

study
studies
studying
is study

Resolução:

No Simple Present, quando o sujeito é 3ª pessoa do singular (He, She, It), adicionamos "s", "es" ou "ies" ao verbo. Para "study", mudamos para studies.

Resposta correta: B

2. Which sentence is in the Present Continuous (action happening now)?

I play soccer on Saturdays.
They are watching a movie right now.
We like pizza very much.
He works in a big office.

Resolução:

O Present Continuous é formado pelo verbo to be (am/is/are) + verbo com -ing. A frase "They are watching" indica algo que ocorre no momento da fala.

Resposta correta: B

3. What is the correct negative form of: "He likes chocolate"?

He not likes chocolate.
He don't like chocolate.
He doesn't like chocolate.
He doesn't likes chocolate.

Resolução:

Para fazer a negativa na 3ª pessoa (He/She/It), usamos o auxiliar doesn't e o verbo volta para a forma original (sem o "s").

Resposta correta: C
