English - Conditionals (If-clauses) 🔀

1. Complete the First Conditional sentence:
"If it _______ tomorrow, we _______ to the beach."

rains - won't go
rain - don't go
will rain - wouldn't go
rained - won't go

Resolução:

A **First Conditional** trata de possibilidades reais. A estrutura é: **If + Simple Present**, **will + verbo**. Portanto: "If it rains... we won't go".

Resposta correta: A

2. In the Second Conditional (imaginary situations), what is the correct form:
"If I _______ rich, I _______ a private jet."

am - will buy
was - would bought
were - would buy
had been - would buy

Resolução:

A **Second Conditional** usa **If + Simple Past**, **would + verbo**. Curiosidade: para o verbo "to be", usamos were para todas as pessoas (I, he, she, it) em contextos formais de condicional.

Resposta correta: C

3. Which sentence represents a Third Conditional (regret about the past)?

If I study, I will pass.
If I had studied more, I would have passed the exam.
If I studied more, I would pass.
I pass if I study.

Resolução:

A **Third Conditional** fala de algo que não aconteceu no passado. Estrutura: **If + Past Perfect (had + part.)**, **would have + part.**.

Resposta correta: B
